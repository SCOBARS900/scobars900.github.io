Information and Instructions

  • This interface was designed to track the conditions of landing spots which includes maps, weather conditions and elevation of land which are importants factors to consider when you're landing.
  • If you want to know how is the proccess to land the spaceship from space until Earth ground, a pilot from NASA gave a nice presentation about it. Click Here
  • The three locations that we're monitoring were used as landing sites for spaceships in the past. The White Sands is an emergency landing site use as a backup when the others are unavaiable. Click Here
  • The maps are interactive and have a custom style. To switch styles between Satellite and Space just click in those options on the map. If any weather information is missing try to reload the page.
